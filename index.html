🧩 My Design Process

  1. Understand – Identify user needs, project goals, and technical constraints through discussion, basic research, and observation.
  2. Plan & Sketch – Create quick wireframes or layout drafts (digital or paper) to explore possible structures and flows.
  3. Visual Design – Develop clean, high-fidelity mockups with attention to usability, hierarchy, and consistency.
  4. Review & Refine – Share with teammates or users to get feedback; revise designs based on insights and actual use cases.
  5. Handoff & Support – Prepare design assets and communicate clearly with developers to ensure smooth implementation—staying involved to adjust designs as needed during development.
